isPrime :: Integer -> Bool
If the lowest divisor of a number is itself, it is prime
pfacs :: Integer -> [Integer]
Returns prime factors for some number. The prime factors of 42 are 2, 3, and 7 and the prime factors of 74 are 2 and 37
primes_by_lcd :: [Integer]
Brute force procedure for generating prime numbers
sieve :: [Integer] -> [Integer]
Procedure for generating primes using the Sieve of Eratosthenes. Starting from 2 cross off all of its multiples from the list of integers. The next number you find will be prime. Cross off all of its multiples, repeat. The numbers left will all be prime
primes_by_wheel :: [Integer]
Taken from http://www.haskell.org/haskellwiki/Prime_numbers This is more or less the brute force method above, but we don't even consider multiples of the first 10 prime numbers
